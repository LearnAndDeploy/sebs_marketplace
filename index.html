About SEBS

The Problem

The rapid growth of mobile technology and digital platforms has transformed how people buy and sell goods and services, particularly among young populations such as university students. On university campuses, students often rely on informal networks, word-of-mouth, or scattered social media posts to access essential services such as food delivery, laundry, printing, tutoring, fashion, repairs, and other student-led businesses. These methods are often inefficient, unreliable, and time-consuming.

Our Solution

SEBS (Student E-Business Space) is a campus-based digital marketplace app designed specifically for students and service providers on the University of Cape Coast (UCC) campus. The app brings together multiple student entrepreneurs and campus businesses onto a single platform, enabling efficient buying, selling, and service exchange.

Market Need

SEBS addresses a real and existing problem on campus: students struggle to easily find trusted vendors for food, services, and products. At the same time, student entrepreneurs lack a structured digital platform to reach customers. The app bridges this gap efficiently, creating a win-win ecosystem for both buyers and sellers on campus.
